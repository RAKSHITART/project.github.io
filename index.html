About the Project

Course Project: City Plan and Algorithmic Applications For our course project, we were tasked with designing a city plan. Our team chose Pune as the city, envisioning it as a modern and efficiently planned urban space. The city was divided into distinct sectors, including: Commercial Sector Small-Scale and Large-Scale Industries Organic Farming Areas Residential Zones We also estimated the costs required to develop the city and outlined the phases in which the construction would proceed. As part of the project, we needed to identify specific areas where course algorithms could be applied. Given Pune's status as a growing hi-tech city, we chose mall management as our primary focus. Malls are crucial in such cities, and efficient management and navigation systems are essential to enhance visitor experiences. Our team collaborated to identify several business cases related to mall management, allowing us to implement the algorithms we studied into real-world scenarios. Each team member worked on specific cases, and details about these cases can be found in their respective profiles.
