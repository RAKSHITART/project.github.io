City Design Project

Mall Management System

Our group decided to take up the mall management system, and we have different business cases. I’m working on:

1. Parking Lot Management System

Purpose: This system assigns parking slots to customers based on their proximity to mall entrances to optimize convenience.

Implementation Details:

  • Selection Sort: Prepares a ranked list of parking lots by distance.
  • Breadth-First Search (BFS): Dynamically assigns the nearest available slot to a visitor.

2. Customer Navigation System

Purpose: Helps guide customers along the shortest path between two points in the mall.

Implementation Details:

  • Graph Representation: Nodes represent locations, and edges represent paths.
  • Dijkstra's Algorithm: Calculates the shortest path between two points.
  • Binary Search Tree: Stores shop locations for efficient lookups.
